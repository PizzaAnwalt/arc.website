The two who started it.

How it started

We got tired of
running alone.

Julian and Kagithan have been friends for years. Somewhere along the way Julian talked Kagithan into lacing up too, and jogging became something we both loved — just never together. Same hobby, same town, two separate routes.

The whole “let's start a run club” idea, though, was pure hot air — endless reels back and forth on Instagram, plenty of hype, and zero follow-through.

Until one day we stopped talking about it. We picked a time, a place, and a first Sunday — and this time we actually showed up.

What keeps us going is Olten itself. We missed doing things together here as a community — the town can feel closed off and a little lonely. The Aare Run Club is our small fix for that: connecting sport and community, one run at a time.
